# KI-003 - Handling cut from transfer contact
Defect type: cut
Symptom: A narrow, more linear mark appears on the shell surface with sharper edges than a natural crack. The mark may be shallow but visually distinct from normal shell texture.
Likely root cause: Contact with a hard transfer point, chute edge, or misaligned handling component can scrape the shell as product moves between stations.
Recommended action / SOP: Inspect transfer points for burrs, sharp edges, exposed fasteners, or misalignment. Clean the path, remove sharp contact points, and re-run a small sample while observing the transfer.
Severity: medium
