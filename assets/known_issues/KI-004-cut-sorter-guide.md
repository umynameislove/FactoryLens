# KI-004 - Sharp-edge contact at sorter guide
Defect type: cut
Symptom: Multiple units show a similar straight or curved cut-like mark in a consistent orientation. The defect can cluster after sorting, singulation, or lane-guiding steps.
Likely root cause: A guide rail, separator, or sorter insert may be contacting the product at one repeatable point. The consistency of the mark is the main clue.
Recommended action / SOP: Stop and inspect guide surfaces in the suspect lane, especially recent replacement parts. Check for rough edges, product rubbing, and lane pressure. Confirm the issue by comparing samples before and after the sorter.
Severity: medium
