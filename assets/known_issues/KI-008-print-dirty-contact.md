# KI-008 - Repeated print-like mark from dirty contact surface
Defect type: print
Symptom: Several units show a similar print-like mark with repeated shape, position, or orientation. The surface is marked but not physically opened.
Likely root cause: A dirty guide, belt, wheel, or fixture can stamp residue repeatedly as units pass. Repetition across units points to a contact surface rather than random contamination.
Recommended action / SOP: Trace marked units back through the line and inspect contact surfaces for matching residue patterns. Clean or replace the suspect component and verify with a short controlled run.
Severity: medium
