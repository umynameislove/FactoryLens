# KI-002 - Compression crack during sizing
Defect type: crack
Symptom: The shell shows a crack with slight edge separation or crushed texture near the split. The defect may appear on units that passed through sizing, feeding, or pressing equipment.
Likely root cause: Excessive compression, narrow guide spacing, or a jammed feed path can load the shell beyond its tolerance. The crack may be linked to a station where product is constrained between moving surfaces.
Recommended action / SOP: Check recent logs for force, gap, or jam-related findings; inspect rollers, belts, and guides for tight spots; run a small controlled sample after adjustment; and compare crack rate before releasing the line.
Severity: high
