# KI-010 - Knife nick from trimming station
Defect type: cut
Symptom: A short straight nick or shallow slice appears near one side of the shell. The edge looks sharper and cleaner than a natural crack, and nearby shells may show marks at a similar angle.
Likely root cause: A trimming blade, guide edge, or misaligned metal lip may be contacting the product path. This is more likely after blade replacement, guide cleaning, or a jam clearance.
Recommended action / SOP: Stop and inspect the trimming station and nearby guides for burrs, exposed sharp edges, or loose fasteners. Remove the affected batch window for review, clean the contact point, and run a small verification sample before returning to full speed.
Severity: high
