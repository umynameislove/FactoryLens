# KI-011 - Pinhole from shell weak spot
Defect type: hole
Symptom: A small round pinhole is visible on the shell surface without a large surrounding chip. The area may look darker because the camera sees into the shell or kernel gap.
Likely root cause: Natural shell weakness, thin shell formation, or a small void can open during handling. It may not come from a machine strike if there is no matching scrape or impact mark nearby.
Recommended action / SOP: Compare the defect rate across lots and suppliers before changing line settings. If pinholes cluster in one lot, separate the lot for quality review and document the supplier/date code. If they appear suddenly across lots, inspect handling drops and transfer height.
Severity: medium
