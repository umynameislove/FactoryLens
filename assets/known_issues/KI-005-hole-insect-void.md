# KI-005 - Insect or kernel void hole
Defect type: hole
Symptom: A localized dark opening or round void appears on the shell. The region can look recessed compared with the surrounding surface and may not align with a mechanical contact direction.
Likely root cause: Natural material defects, insect damage, or a pre-existing void can create a hole-like appearance. The defect may originate before the current production step.
Recommended action / SOP: Review incoming inspection notes and compare the unit with other samples from the same lot. If holes repeat across the lot, quarantine affected material and escalate to supplier or incoming-quality review.
Severity: high
