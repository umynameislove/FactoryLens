# KI-006 - Impact chip mistaken as hole
Defect type: hole
Symptom: A missing chip creates a dark region that resembles a hole, often with broken shell edges or a small surrounding fracture.
Likely root cause: Drop impact, collision in bulk handling, or aggressive agitation can remove a shell chip. The visual result can be classified as a hole even when the underlying cause is mechanical impact.
Recommended action / SOP: Inspect hoppers, drop heights, and accumulation points for hard impacts. Compare with logs or observations from transfer changes, then reduce impact points or add cushioning where practical.
Severity: medium
