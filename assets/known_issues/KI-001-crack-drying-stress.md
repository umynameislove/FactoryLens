# KI-001 - Drying stress crack
Defect type: crack
Symptom: A thin irregular line crosses the shell surface, often following a natural weak direction. The surrounding surface is not deeply gouged, and the defect can appear as a dry split rather than a fresh cut.
Likely root cause: Moisture loss or uneven drying can create shell stress that opens an existing weak point. Handling after drying may make the crack more visible, but the primary condition is usually material stress rather than a single impact event.
Recommended action / SOP: Review drying and storage conditions for recent lots, compare the affected unit with nearby samples, and inspect whether cracks repeat on similar shell locations. If repeated, hold the lot for process review before changing mechanical settings.
Severity: medium
