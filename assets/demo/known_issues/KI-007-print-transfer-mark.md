# KI-007 - Ink or belt transfer mark
Defect type: print
Symptom: A colored or high-contrast mark sits on the surface without obvious cracking, cutting, or missing material. The mark may look printed, stamped, or transferred.
Likely root cause: Contact with ink, belt residue, marker material, or packaging/label debris can transfer a visible mark onto the shell surface.
Recommended action / SOP: Inspect belts, trays, gloves, labels, and contact surfaces for residue. Clean suspect surfaces, segregate visibly marked units, and verify whether marks disappear after sanitation.
Severity: low
